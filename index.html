🎮 Game Points
How do I earn points? ▼
Every block you hit in the game = 1 point. Your combo multiplier (up to ×3.0) increases the points you get per hit when you chain consecutive blocks without missing the ball.
How much is a point worth in FREE mode? ▼
In FREE mode, 10,000 points = $0.01 (one cent). You need 10,000,000 points to earn $10 (the withdrawal minimum). It's a long grind by design — referrals are the fast lane!
How much is a point worth in PRO mode? ▼
In PRO mode ($5/week), 5,000 points = $0.01. That's exactly 2× the free rate. PRO is worth it for dedicated players who log in daily.
Is there a daily point earning limit? ▼
Yes. FREE users can earn up to 50,000 points per day. PRO users have a cap of 100,000 points per day. Referral earnings have no daily cap.
Do my points ever expire? ▼
No, your points don't expire as long as your account is active. Accounts with zero activity for 365+ days may have points reviewed per our Terms of Service.
👥 Referral System
How exactly do I earn from referrals? ▼
Share your unique referral link. When someone clicks it AND subscribes to PRO ($5/week), you earn $1.00 credited instantly to your wallet. Free sign-ups without a PRO subscription do not count.
What are the milestone bonuses? ▼
10 referrals → +$10 bonus, 50 referrals → +$100 bonus, 100 referrals → +$300 bonus, 250 referrals → +$1,000 bonus. They are cumulative. Total at 100 referrals: $100 + $10 + $100 + $300 = $510.
Can I refer myself with a second account? ▼
No. Self-referrals result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of all earnings. Our system detects duplicate accounts through device fingerprinting, IP tracking, and payment information.
💸 Withdrawals
What is the minimum withdrawal amount? ▼
The minimum withdrawal is $10.00. Your total balance — which includes game point cash value, referral earnings, and milestone bonuses — all count together toward this threshold.
How do I withdraw? ▼
Go to your Wallet tab and tap "Request Withdrawal". Select your crypto network (ETH/ERC20, BNB/BEP20, or TRX/TRC20), enter your wallet address, and submit. Processing takes 5–14 business days.
Are there withdrawal fees? ▼
Withdrawals above $20 are fee-free. For the $10–$20 range, a small $0.50 processing fee applies. Network gas fees may also apply and vary by blockchain.
⚡ PRO Subscription
What exactly does PRO give me? ▼
PRO ($5/week) gives you: 5,000 pts = $0.01 instead of 10,000 (2× earn rate), double daily point cap (100K vs 50K), PRO purple paddle + glowing ball, and priority withdrawal processing.
Can I cancel PRO anytime? ▼
Yes. PRO is week-to-week with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your PRO benefits remain active until the end of your paid week.

📋 Withdrawal Policy
Please read this carefully before requesting a withdrawal. By submitting a request, you agree to these terms.
Minimum Withdrawal

The minimum withdrawal amount is $10.00 USD. Requests below this threshold will be automatically rejected. Your total balance includes game point value, referral earnings, and milestone bonuses combined.

Processing Time

All withdrawal requests are processed manually by the Earnova team. Standard processing time is 5 to 14 business days from the date your request is approved. Business days exclude weekends and public holidays. During high-volume periods, processing may take up to 21 days.

Supported Networks
  • ETH (ERC20) — Ethereum mainnet. Wallet address starts with 0x. Standard gas fees may apply.
  • BNB (BEP20) — Binance Smart Chain. Wallet address starts with 0x. Low gas fees.
  • TRX (TRC20) — TRON network. Wallet address starts with T. Very low fees.
User Responsibility
  • You are solely responsible for providing the correct wallet address and network.
  • Earnova is NOT responsible for funds sent to wrong addresses due to user error.
  • Ensure your wallet supports the selected network before submitting.
  • Withdrawal requests cannot be cancelled once submitted and approved.
Fees

Withdrawals above $20 are fee-free from Earnova. For $10–$20 withdrawals, a $0.50 processing fee is deducted. Blockchain network gas fees are separate and may vary. These are not charged by Earnova.

Right to Refuse

Earnova reserves the right to refuse, delay, or cancel any withdrawal request if we suspect fraud, abuse of referral systems, self-referrals, bot usage, or any violation of our Terms of Service. Accounts found in violation forfeit all earnings.

Changes to Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Earnova constitutes acceptance of the current policy. Last updated: April 2026.
